Royal Cars Rental Policy

Welcome to our Rental Policy page. This section outlines the important terms and conditions that govern our car rental services.

We strongly encourage all our customers to read and understand this policy carefully before completing their rental booking.

The information provided here covers essential details such as eligibility requirements, rental rates and fees, mileage restrictions, damage responsibilities, and our cancellation and refund procedures.

Rental Eligibility

To rent a vehicle from ROYAL CARS, customers must meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 21 years of age (18-20 with young driver fee)
  • Possess a valid driver's license
  • Provide acceptable proof of identity
  • Have a valid credit or debit card for security deposit

We reserve the right to verify the customer's identity, driving record, and insurance coverage before approving the rental.

Rental Rates and Fees

Our transparent pricing includes:

  • Competitive base daily or weekly rates
  • All applicable taxes and surcharges
  • Optional services (GPS, child seats, etc.)
  • Insurance coverage options

All charges will be clearly outlined in your rental agreement prior to final booking. We offer various insurance options including liability, collision damage waiver, and personal accident insurance.

Rental Period and Mileage

Important guidelines regarding rental duration:

  • Vehicles must be returned on scheduled date and time
  • Mileage restrictions apply as per agreement
  • Late returns incur additional charges
  • Excess mileage fees calculated per agreement

Customers requiring extensions should contact our team immediately to avoid additional charges. Modifications are subject to vehicle availability.

Damages and Liability

Customer responsibilities include:

  • Safe operation of the rental vehicle
  • Reporting any damages immediately
  • Financial responsibility for loss or damage
  • Verifying personal insurance coverage

In case of accident or incident, customers must immediately notify our team and provide all necessary documentation. We recommend reviewing your personal auto insurance for adequate rental coverage.

Cancellations and Refunds

Our flexible cancellation policy:

  • Full/partial refunds based on cancellation timing
  • Changes must be made within specified timeframe
  • Contact our team for modifications
  • Refund processing details in agreement

We understand plans may change and strive to accommodate our customers. Please contact us as soon as possible for any reservation changes to ensure eligibility for refunds.

KEY CONSIDERATIONS

This policy constitutes a legally binding agreement between you and Royal Cars. We strongly recommend reviewing your personal insurance coverage before renting. Additional restrictions may apply based on vehicle type and rental location. Please contact our customer service for any clarifications.
